Mila Galloway

The creator of 11th Tower. Galloway created the Tower way, where we individuals, celebrate our purposeless life. She had created the foundation of the 11th Tower, building its principles and outlining it to something actionable. Galloway wrote 3 books about the ways othe 11th Tower, “As much as Possible”, “Get out of your own head”, “The 11th Tower's Doors.” She unfortunately passed away in 2021 due to partying too much.
