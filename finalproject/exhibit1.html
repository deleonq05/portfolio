The people of Chicago

What makes the Chicago people interesting? The different paths they take in everyday life. Created by de Leon, the photograph pieces focuses on Chicagoans. As a Filipino immigrant, de Leon feels as though finding connection within a community can be a formidable force to be reckoned with. But she found that, wherever she is, there will be people that she will come across that will offer her kindness, offer her kinship. She'll meet these people once, maybe twice. Some stay in her life, some doesn't. But it doesn't matter, what matters is that we're all here together, walking the path.
