The Best Filipino Restaurant in Chicago

Talong is a cozy Filipino restaurant nestled in the heart of Chicago, offering an authentic taste of the Philippines with a modern twist. The restaurant's name, meaning "eggplant" in Tagalog, reflects its commitment to highlighting the diverse and vibrant flavors of Filipino cuisine. From savory adobo and sizzling sisig to creamy leche flan, Talong serves up delicious comfort food that will make you feel like you're dining at a friend's house. Whether you're a Filipino food aficionado or a newcomer to the cuisine, Talong promises a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for sharing a meal and creating memories.
