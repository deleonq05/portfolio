About me!

My name is Quinn de Leon. A 21-year-old aspiring to be in the digital marketing field. Constructing designs and using compositions to move people is my calling. If I'm not on the lookout for new company launches or different approaches to rebranding, then I'm using distinct forms of art to channel my creativity. I love video editing and photography! Currently, I study at Columbia College Chicago in pursuit of finishing my bachelor's in User Experience and Interaction Design.
